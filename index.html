About

I work on improving how databases recover after cyberattacks by designing efficient log organization and dependency tracking techniques. My goal is to reduce recovery latency while preserving deterministic correctness. I also explore learning-augmented components that advise performance decisions (e.g., caching/retention) without changing recovery semantics.
